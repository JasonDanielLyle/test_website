About

This app provides a visual overlay of Los Angeles street sweeping schedules to help residents avoid citations and stay informed about neighborhood maintenance. It was made with the goal of always being free and easy to use. Inspired by the SF Street Cleaning Map by Kaushal Partani.

×

Disclaimer

Data is sourced from public GeoJSON records. While we strive for accuracy, schedules can change due to holidays, weather, or city updates. Always check posted street signs as the primary source of truth.
