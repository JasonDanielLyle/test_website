About

Hi! I made this visual overlay of Los Angeles street sweeping schedules to help residents avoid citations and stay informed about neighborhood maintenance. Select a neighborhood, select your street, and add reminders right to your calendar!

I will be adding more streets and neighborhoods as I have time.
